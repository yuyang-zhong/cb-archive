## Overview
The **CC&Rs (Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions)** is a comprehensive, legally binding agreement that dictates the specific rules of living in the 148-unit development.

## Important Sections

### Affordable Housing Program
A defining feature of the CC&Rs is the integration of the City's Limited Equity Program (LEP) (Section 2.15). This mandates that condominiums must be the owner's primary residence for at least ten months out of the year (Section 3.1) and enforces strict limitations on renting units (Section 3.2). 

### Community Rules & Restrictions
Article 3 outlines the community's standards for daily living, including:
*   **Noise & Conduct:** (Sections 2.13, 3.14)
*   **Pets:** (Section 3.6)
*   **Technology:** Drone usage (Section 3.17) and solar panel installation (Section 3.18).
*   **Parking:** Limited, leased subject to availability, and regulated by City Parking Policies (Section 2.10 and Exhibit C). 

### Maintenance Responsibilities
Article 4 clearly divides property upkeep:
*   **Association:** Maintains common areas and exteriors (Section 4.2).
*   **Homeowners:** Responsible for maintaining their unit's interiors and exclusive use patios (Section 4.1).

### Assessments & Insurance
Article 6 details the homeowner's obligation to pay regular and special assessments (Sections 6.1, 6.2, 6.4). Article 8 explains the Association's "bare walls" property insurance policy (Section 8.1.1) and emphasizes that **homeowners must purchase their own individual HO-6 insurance policies** to cover personal property and unit interiors (Section 8.3).
