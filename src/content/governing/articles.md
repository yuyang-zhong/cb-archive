## Overview
The Articles of Incorporation act as the community’s legal birth certificate. Filed in September 2024, this document officially establishes the **China Basin Homeowners Association**.

## Key Legal Provisions

### Legal Establishment
This document officially establishes the Association as a registered California Nonprofit Mutual Benefit Corporation.

### Primary Mission
The sole purpose of the corporation is to manage, administer, and maintain the 400 China Basin Condominiums.

### Core Authority
The Articles grant the Association its fundamental legal powers, including the authority to levy and collect assessments and to promote the health, safety, and welfare of all its members.

### Operating Limits
The Association is strictly prohibited from engaging in any business or activities that do not directly further this specific community management purpose.
