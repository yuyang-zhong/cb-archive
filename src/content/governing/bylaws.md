## Overview
The **Bylaws** provide the operational manual for how the Association is run on a day-to-day basis, focusing on leadership and member rights.

## Governance Highlights

### Board of Directors & Officers
Article 6 establishes a Board of Directors—initially consisting of three members and expanding to five—who are elected by the homeowners (Sections 6.1, 6.2). Article 9 defines the duties of key officers appointed by the Board, including the President, Secretary, and Chief Financial Officer (Sections 9.1, 9.2, 9.6).

### Homeowner Meetings & Voting
Article 5 outlines how and when community meetings occur, specifying the rules for the annual meeting (Section 5.2), special meetings (Section 5.3), notice requirements (Section 5.4), quorum minimums (Section 5.8), and voting proxies (Section 5.9). 

### Transparency & Due Process
The Bylaws mandate that the Board follow specific due process requirements before penalizing a homeowner (Section 8.5). They also ensure residents have the right to inspect Association records (Section 12.4) and review Board meeting minutes (Section 7.14).
