## Overview
SuperClean Building Services provides comprehensive janitorial support for the community. The contract specifies a detailed checklist for all levels of the building.

## Service Schedule
One part-time personnel is assigned to the property from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM on service days.

## Scope of Work
*   **Daily (on service days):** Lobby cleaning, trash room disinfection, and hallway vacuuming.
*   **Amenities:** Deep cleaning of the 8th-floor community room, fitness center, and roof top area.
