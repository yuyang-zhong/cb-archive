## Overview
Action Property Management serves as the exclusive managing agent for the China Basin Community Association. This comprehensive agreement covers all aspects of community operations.

## Key Service Areas

### 1. Financial Management
Agent establishes and maintains operating and reserve accounts, conducts assessment billing, and prepares monthly financial statements for Board review.

### 2. Physical Management
Includes regular on-site inspections (once a month) and supervision of third-party subcontractors for repairs and maintenance.

### 3. Administrative Services
Agent manages Association records, coordinates Board meetings and annual membership meetings, and handles communication with homeowners.
