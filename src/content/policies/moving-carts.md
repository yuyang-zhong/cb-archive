## Overview
Before utilizing the community's utility carts, Residents must sign a liability waiver releasing the Association from any claims related to cart usage.

## Rules of Use
*   **Weight Limits:** Carts are to be used for carrying items only; they are not built for heavy furniture.
*   **Safety:** Absolutely no one is allowed to ride the carts. Children are explicitly forbidden from being in or on them.
*   **Age:** Users must be at least 16 years of age.
*   **Return:** Carts must be returned to the garage immediately after use in a clean condition.
