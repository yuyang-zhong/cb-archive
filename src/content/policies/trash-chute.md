## Overview
This policy outlines exact requirements for disposing of refuse to prevent equipment damage, clogs, and sanitary hazards in the trash chutes.

## Disposal Rules
*   **Cardboard:** Cardboard boxes of any kind are strictly forbidden from being placed in the chute. All boxes must be flattened and hand-carried to the ground floor trash room.
*   **Bagging:** All trash and compost must be double-tied in leak-proof bags before being placed in the chute.
*   **Prohibited Items:** Styrofoam, liquids, loose food, and heavy or bulky items are entirely prohibited from entering the chutes.
