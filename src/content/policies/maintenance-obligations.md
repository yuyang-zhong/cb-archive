## Overview
This file uses an easy-to-read matrix to clearly define whether the Unit Owner or the Association is financially and operationally responsible for maintaining, repairing, or replacing specific property components.

## Association Responsibilities
The Association is responsible for all exterior building components, including the roof, foundation, exterior paint, rain gutters, and common area lighting.

## Unit Owner Responsibilities
The Unit Owner is responsible for maintaining their interior components, such as:
*   Appliances and cabinets.
*   Floor coverings.
*   Interior plumbing systems.
*   Battery replacements for smoke/carbon monoxide detectors.

## Window Policy
While windows are expected to last the life of the building, if they must be repaired or replaced, the Association will perform the work, but the Unit Owner must reimburse the Association for the cost.
