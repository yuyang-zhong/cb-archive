## Overview
This policy establishes the monetary penalties for violations of the community's Governing Documents, utilizing a progressive warning system. 

## Fine Schedule
The standard schedule begins with three zero-dollar ($0.00) warnings. On the fourth identical offense, the Owner is called to a hearing and may be fined $100 for that and any subsequent identical offenses.

## Safety Exception
A crucial "Safety Exception" allows the Board to bypass warnings and immediately impose fines greater than $100 for violations that create an adverse health or safety impact. 

**Immediate Fines Apply to:**
*   Smoking or vaping in common areas.
*   Hoarding that attracts pests.
*   Setting off fireworks or pulling prank fire alarms.
*   Speeding in the garage.
