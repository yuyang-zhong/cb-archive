## Overview
This document establishes the property as a strict non-smoking and non-vaping building to prevent fire hazards and secondhand smoke exposure.

## Prohibited Areas
Smoking and vaping (of tobacco, marijuana, or any other plant/weed) is entirely prohibited in all common areas, including the roof deck, garage, lobbies, and within 20 feet of the main entrance.

## Medical Accommodations
The Board may grant a "Reasonable Accommodation" for a resident to smoke medical THC within their private unit if authorized by a medical professional.

## Nuisance Rule
If a medically accommodated resident's smoke or odor triggers more than three complaints from neighbors (constituting a "nuisance"), the resident must install smoke extraction systems at their own expense to maintain the accommodation.
