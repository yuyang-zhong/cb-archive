## Overview
This document governs the reservation and daily use of community recreational spaces, including the 8th Floor Clubhouse, Yoga/Play Room, Business Center, Pet Wash Room, and Roof Deck. 

## Clubhouse Reservations
To reserve the Clubhouse, an Owner must submit a $100 non-refundable usage fee and a $350 refundable damage deposit. A reservation cannot exceed five hours.

## Other Amenities
The Yoga/Play Room and Business Center can be reserved without a fee or deposit, provided:
*   No food or drinks (other than water) are served.
*   The reservation is kept to a maximum of two (Yoga) or four (Business) hours.

## Pet Rules
Pets are strictly prohibited in all amenity spaces, except for legally required service animals. Failure to clean up pet waste in these areas incurs a $200 fine.
