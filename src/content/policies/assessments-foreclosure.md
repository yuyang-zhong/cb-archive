## Overview
This policy serves as the annual statement outlining the Association's collection procedures and the rights of the Owners. 

## Delinquency Timeline
Assessments are officially considered delinquent if not paid within 15 days of the due date. 

## Foreclosure Limitations
The Association may pursue judicial or nonjudicial foreclosure only if the delinquent assessment debt (excluding late charges and fees) exceeds $1,800 or is more than 12 months delinquent. 

## Owner Rights
Owners have the right to:
*   Request a receipt for their payments.
*   Request a meeting with the Board to discuss a payment plan. 
*   Dispute charges by paying under protest.
*   Formally request Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) before foreclosure is initiated.
