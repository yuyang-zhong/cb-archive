## Overview
This document strictly regulates the distribution and usage of leased parking licenses for Below Market Rate (BMR) owners within the community.

## Waitlist & Lottery
BMR parking licenses are offered via a lottery rank order. If demand exceeds supply, a "BMR Parking Waitlist" is created.

## Sale of Unit
When a BMR unit with a parking space is sold, the parking license is returned to the pool and offered to the next person on the waitlist; the new buyer is not automatically entitled to the space.

## Prohibitions
BMR owners are strictly prohibited from subleasing their parking rights to any other person.

## Market-Rate Usage
If no BMR owners want the space, the Master HOA can seek permission from the Mayor's Office of Housing and Community Development (MOHCD) to lease the space to a market-rate owner on a month-to-month basis.
