## Overview
This file ensures that Association membership meetings and Board of Director elections are conducted fairly and in accordance with California law. 

## Eligibility
To be eligible to run for or serve on the Board, a Candidate must be current on all regular and special assessments and cannot hold a joint ownership interest with another candidate or incumbent director.

## Voting Process
All voting for Directors must be conducted solely by Secret Ballot; a show of hands or voice vote is prohibited. 
*   **The Double Envelope:** The process requires the ballot to be sealed inside an anonymous ballot envelope, which is then sealed inside an outer address envelope signed by the Member.
*   **Inspectors:** The Board must appoint one or three independent Inspector(s) of Election to receive, verify, and tabulate the ballots.
