## Introduction
These Rules and Regulations serve as a guide for all Members, tenants, and guests of the China Basin Community. They are designed to ensure the maximum enjoyment of the premises without interference, focusing on safety, order, and community reputation.

## 1. Living Standards & Use Restrictions
*   **Residential Use:** All units are for residential purposes only. Home-based businesses are only permitted if they comply with local jurisdiction and do not create a nuisance.
*   **Smoking & Vaping:** Entirely prohibited in all Common Areas (lobbies, hallways, roof deck, garage, etc.). Smoking within units is allowed only if smoke is prevented from migrating to common areas or neighboring units.
*   **Disturbing Noise:** Residents are entitled to quiet enjoyment. No noise or vibrations should be discernable to other residents. Specifically, speakers must not be affixed to any walls or ceilings to prevent vibration transfer.

## 2. Pet Policies
*   **Registration:** All pets must be registered within 10 days of occupancy or acquisition.
*   **Limits:** Maximum of two (2) dogs or cats. Fish in aquariums are limited to a 30-gallon maximum.
*   **Restraint:** Pets must be on a leash at all times in Common Areas. Pets are not allowed in amenity spaces (Business Center, Club Room, Yoga Room, etc.).
*   **Prohibited Breeds:** "Fighting breeds" such as Pit Bulls, Rottweilers, Doberman Pinschers, and Mastiffs are not allowed on the premises.

## 3. Moving & Deliveries
*   **Scheduling:** Moves must be scheduled with the move-in coordinator. Moves are limited to three per day (morning, afternoon, early evening).
*   **Hours:** Monday through Saturday, 9:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. No moves on Sundays or Holidays.
*   **Security Deposit:** A refundable $250 deposit is required. Owners are responsible for any damage exceeding this amount.
*   **Large Deliveries:** Must be coordinated through the management office and use the designated street-front delivery entrance.

## 4. Building Appearance & Maintenance
*   **Windows & Patios:** Window coverings must have a white or off-white lining visible from the exterior. Patios must be kept clean and free of debris; storage of bicycles, boxes, or barbeques is not permitted on patios.
*   **Air Filters:** Units are equipped with MERV filters to protect against air pollutants. Owners are responsible for replacing these every 6–12 months.
*   **Flooring:** Any hardwood or tile installation requires prior Board approval and must meet specific "Noise Insulation Standards."

## 5. Security & Access
*   **Keys & Fobs:** Do not loan building access keys or fobs to vendors or delivery personnel. Lost keys incur a $25 fee, and garage remotes incur a $40 fee.
*   **Doors:** Propping open Common Area or unit entry doors is a fire hazard and is strictly prohibited.
*   **Building Access:** The main entry is key/fob controlled for security. Owners are responsible for the conduct of their guests and tenants.

## 6. Garbage & Recycling
*   **Chute Rules:** All items must be bagged in leak-proof, double-tied containers.
*   **Cardboard:** Boxes are strictly forbidden in the chutes. They must be flattened and carried to the ground floor trash room.
*   **Hazardous Materials:** Volatile materials, glass, or heavy objects must never be placed in the chutes as they can damage equipment or cause injury to maintenance staff.

## 7. Vehicle & Parking
*   **Designated Spaces:** Only residents with valid parking licenses may use the garage. Spaces are not assigned and are available on a first-come, first-served basis within the licensed areas.
*   **Restrictions:** No recreational vehicles, boats, or commercial trucks. Vehicle washing and maintenance (other than emergency work) are prohibited in the garage.
*   **Electric Vehicles:** EV chargers are for the convenience of licensed residents only. Non-EVs must not park in designated charging stalls.
