## Overview
This document establishes the procedures and design standards for any Owner planning to make modifications to their unit. 

## Application Process
Owners must submit an application package that includes:
*   Property Improvement Form
*   $50 application fee
*   Detailed construction plans

The Architectural Committee (ACC) has up to 60 days to review the submission. Once work is finished, an Owner has 30 days to submit a Notice of Completion.

## Design & Noise Standards
For noise mitigation, any hard surface flooring installation requires an underlayment that achieves a Sound Transmission Class (STC) rating of at least 54 and an Impact Insulation Class (IIC) rating of at least 52. 

## Contractor Rules
Contractors are strictly limited to working between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. They are not permitted access on weekends or holidays.
